To Ayesha, a blessing from Allah 💫

Alhamdulillah, for the beautiful gift that is you. In a world full of distractions, Allah has guided my heart to someone whose character shines with sincerity, compassion, and faith. You remind me daily of the Prophet ﷺ’s words: “The best among you are those who are best to their wives.” And though we are not yet married, I strive to treat you with the honor and kindness that Islam teaches.

Your presence brings tranquility to my soul, just as Allah describes in the Qur’an:

“And among His signs is that He created for you from yourselves mates that you may find tranquility in them, and He placed between you affection and mercy.”
(Surah Ar-Rum 30:21)

You are a reflection of that mercy and affection. Your patience, your modesty, your strength in faith—all of it inspires me to be a better man, not just for you, but for the sake of Allah.

May Allah bless you, protect your heart, and grant us both the ability to walk this path with taqwa, love, and purpose. I pray that our bond grows in righteousness and that one day, insha’Allah, we are united in a way that pleases our Creator.
